About

Find Out More About Us

Ohms Electronics Pvt. Ltd. is one of the leading supplier of Servers, Networking Equipments & their Spare Parts of all leading brands including HP, DELL, IBM, SUN & CISCO

We offer high end IT hardware at reasonable prices. All our products are tested for quality by our certified technicians to ensure uninterrupted performance, longer service life and resistance against abrasion. To ensure the same, we procure these servers and spare parts from the most trusted vendors of the industry such as HP, IBM and Dell.

We have been able to establish ourselves as one of the respectable organizations in this domain owing to the support of our reliable vendors. These vendors are shortlisted by our experienced procuring agents on the basis of their experience, financial stability, sound infrastructure and market credibility. Moreover, our vendors make use of only premium quality components for the manufacturing of offered HP, SUN, IBM & DELL Servers and Spare Parts while keeping in mind the internationally followed quality standards of the industry.

Ohms Electronics Pvt. Ltd. offers a wide spectrum of services related to control systems (DCS, PLC, SCADA, etc.), field instrumentation, networking and Special Instruments.

With our industrial expertise, we have been able to establish ourselves as an eminent wholesaler, supplier, retailer and distributor of a commendable array of HP, SUN, IBM & DELL Servers and Spare Parts. To ensure the satisfaction of patrons, we provide these servers and spare parts as per their given details. Moreover, we strictly follow ethical business policies so as to maintain the hard-earned trust of patrons.
